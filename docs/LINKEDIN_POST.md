## 📋 The post — copy everything below

🚀 From a problem statement to a working full-stack product — and a live demo to an industry expert. Meet **LoanLens**.

For **PS-01: Loan EMI Eligibility Pre-Screener**, our team built a tool that tells a bank's call-centre agent, in **ONE second**, whether a loan applicant can qualify.

🏦 **The problem**
Agents waste 10–15 minutes on applicants who can never qualify. Banks judge eligibility using the **FOIR** rule (Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio) — the share of monthly income already going to loan EMIs. We put that rule right at the agent's fingertips.

✨ **What LoanLens does**
✅ Instant verdict — Eligible / Borderline / Not-eligible, with a reason to read to the customer
🎯 Smart Counter-Offer — turns a "no" into the largest loan they CAN get (or the tenure that qualifies)
🎚️ Live "what-if" sliders — drag and watch the eligibility dial move in real time
🔀 One-click comparison across all loan products
📊 Analytics dashboard — approval rate, average FOIR, verdict split
🔐 Secure & full-stack — sign-up / login, hashed passwords, a live database

🛠️ **Built with**
A pure **Core-Java** engine (24 JUnit tests ✅) · **Spring Boot 3** · **Spring Security + H2** · HTML / CSS / JS · Maven.
We focused on the fundamentals — precise decimal maths, banker's rounding, input validation, and zero hard-coded rates.

🎤 **A special highlight:** we got to present LoanLens live to an **industry expert** — and the feedback was genuinely invaluable. Grateful for the chance to demo our work and learn from someone in the field. 🙌
