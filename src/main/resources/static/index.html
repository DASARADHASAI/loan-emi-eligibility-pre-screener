What is FOIR?

Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio.

The share of a person's monthly income already committed to loan EMIs. A bank pre-approves when this stays under its limit — because too much of the salary is already spoken for.

FOIR = (existing EMIs + new loan EMI) ÷ monthly income

Example: income ₹1,00,000, total EMIs ₹47,000 → FOIR = 47% → within a 50% limit → eligible.

≤ 50%Eligible
50–55%Borderline · review
